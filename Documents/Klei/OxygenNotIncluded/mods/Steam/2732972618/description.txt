[h1][b]Clean Deconstruction[/b][/h1]
Keep the remaining gas and liquid in bottles instead of making a mess when a building is deconstructed.


[h1][b]整洁拆除[/b][/h1]
拆除建筑时，内部剩余的气体和液体以瓶装形式掉出，而不是直接倒在原地一团糟。
